GGF10 - The Tenth Global Grid Forum
March 9 – 13, 2004
Berlin, Germany
Humboldt University

Special Thanks to Humboldt University & our local coordinator, Alexander Reinefeld, Humboldt University & Zuse Institute!

The meeting gathered ~600 grid enthusiasts, practitioners and experts. The first telecom meetings was also held during this meeting.
