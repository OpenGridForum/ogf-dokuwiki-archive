Next Generation Data Center Event

Join the Open Grid Forum at Next Generation Data Center Event!

NGDC is a new event being held August 6-9, 2007 at Moscone Center in San Francisco. Focused on emerging technologies in the IT infrastructure, NGDC is designed for IT Architects determining how they will transform their data center to be more virtual and dynamic. A full conference and trade show (including LinuxWorld and Enterprise Information World), NGDC will have over 10,000 attendees, 200 exhibitors, and 100 conference sessions!

The Open Grid Forum will be there, and has negotiated the following benefits for OGF members:

  • Organizational Members exhibiting in the Grid Pavilion get a complete turnkey package for $6,000. All members exhibiting in the event receive extra exposure in the Show Guide and show promotion as an Open Grid Forum member, 2 complimentary conference passes, and 50 pre-qualified conference attendee leads with full contact information.
  • OGF Members also receive a 20% discount on any NGDC conference pass. Simply register at www.ngdcexpo.com using the promotion code: H0103.

For information about exhibiting, please contact Amy Bonner (Companies A-L) at 508-424-4892 or Christian Montminy (Companies M-Z) at 508-424-4852. Other event information may be found at www.ngdcexpo.com.
