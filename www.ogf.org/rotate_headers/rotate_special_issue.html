OGF Special Journal Issue:
Practice and Experience for Open Grid Forum

Submissions are being sought for a Special Journal Issue of Special Issue of Concurrency and Computation: Practice and Experience for the Open Grid Forum.

Editors: Craig Lee and Geoffrey Fox
  • Each paper will be reviewed by at least three peers.
  • Please submit paper to Manuscript Central as OGF2007 special issue.
  • Papers are solicited on international status of research, standards or application in areas covered by OGF councils or groups. The topics of informational documents associated with groups would be in scope.
  • Papers would be refereed as normal but referees would be informed as to guidelines so papers need not be breakthrough research but could describe standards, interoperability, experience or review best practice.
  • All papers must be formally endorsed by an OGF group or council as to its relevance to OGF. This endorsement (affiliation) should be indicated in submittal letter
  • Papers need not represent consensus view of council or group.

Timeline:
  • Papers due March 15, 2008
  • Review results April 15, 2008
  • Final papers due May 15, 2008
  • Publication 4 months after last final paper submitted
