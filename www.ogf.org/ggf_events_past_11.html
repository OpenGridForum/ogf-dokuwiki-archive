Past GGF Events

GGF11 - The Eleventh Global Grid Forum
June 6 – 10, 2004
Honolulu, Hawaii, USA
Hilton Hawaiian Village Beach Resort & Spa
The meeting gathered ~500 grid enthusiasts, practitioners and experts.
 
GGF11 Participants
GGF11 Schedule

Keynote Speakers:
Hiro Kishimoto and Ian Foster – Presentation “Open Grid Services Architecture Rising”

Hiro Kishimto - Dr. Hiro Kishimoto is a Research Fellow of GRID Computing & Bioinformatics Laboratory, Fujitsu Laboratories Limited.
 
Ian Foster - Associate Division Director, Senior Computer Scientist - Head, Distributed Systems Laboratory - Computational grid, distributed computing technologies, Globus, climate modeling

Jamie Clark - Manager for Technical Standards Development, provides direct assistance to the operations of OASIS technical committees.

Yoshio Tanaka – Presentation “Asia Pacific Grid Towards a Production Research Grid”
AIST Grid Technology Research Center, National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology
 
Invited speakers:

  • Roger Reich, DMTF
  • Philippe Le Hegaret, W3C
  • Jamie Clark, OASIS
  • Adrian Cockcroft, EGA
  • David Snelling, GGF

Special Presentations
David Snelling (Fujitsu) - WSRF/WSN Update

Lessons Learned in Research Grid Deployments (Research Panel)

  • Vincent Breton (CNRS) - DataGrid, EGEE and LCG
  • Malcolm Atkinson (UK National e-Science Centre) - DataGrid, EGEE and LCG
  • Robert Gardner (University of Chicago) - Grid3
  • Laura Pearlman (ISI) – NEESgrid
