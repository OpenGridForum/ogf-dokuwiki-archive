Sponsorship Prospectus

The 20th Open Grid Forum - OGF20/EGEE 2nd User Forum
May 7 - 11, 2007
Manchester Central
Manchester, UK

Sponsorship opportunities are now available for OGF20/EGEE User Forum. This event includes an exhibit area where you can showcase your products and network with potential buyers. It also features a two day enterprise and industry program, led in conjunction with Grid Computing Now!, that will focus on real world case studies and practical Grid solutions for business. Take this unique opportunity to gain exposure for your company and products with our highly Grid focused audience.

SPONSORSHIP BENEFITS:

  • Targeted audience of more than 800 Grid technical and management leaders, researchers and Grid users from around the globe
  • Unique opportunity to gain exposure for your company and products in the sponsor exhibit area
  • Gain access to the rapidly growing scientific Grid user community at the EGEE User Forum
  • Several exclusive offers to capture audience attention including receptions for OGF and EGEE attendees and OGF co-branded promotional items
  • Chair appreciation night sponsorship targeting the top 100 OGF technical leaders
  • Extensive networking opportunities at sessions and social events

Sponsorship opportunities include booth space in the vendor showcase area as well as exclusive sponsorships for receptions, promotional items, lanyards, etc. Click here to view the OGF20 Sponsor Prospectus and to get all the details.
