Lodging

The 20th Open Grid Forum - OGF20/EGEE 2nd User Forum
May 7 - 11, 2007
Manchester Central
Manchester, UK

OGF has secured blocks of sleeping rooms through Marketing Manchester at a variety of hotels in the City Centre area of Manchester. Each of these hotels is within walking distance of the Manchester Central Convention Center, as well as a wide variety of restaurants, pubs and shops. With the exception of the IBIS Hotel which is room and tax only, the rates listed on the Marketing Manchester web site include tax and a full English breakfast. To book your hotel reservation, please click on the link to new reservations below where you will be directly connected to the Marketing Manchester reservation site. At that point you will enter Event Code: MNCOGF20 to view hotel options and complete the reservation process.

Derya Nielsen & Sylvia Herring with Marketing Manchester will be our event housing contacts, and any questions or changes to your hotel reservations should be directed to their attention. Their contact information is listed below.

Karen Kus with OGF is available to answer any other questions you might have with regard to this event, and her contact information has been provided below as well.
