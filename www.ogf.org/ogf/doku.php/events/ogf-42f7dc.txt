OGF will meet with all appropriate working and community groups(*) at the [[http://autonomic-conference.org|International Conference on Cloud and Autonomic Computing (CAC 2014)]] to take place September 8-12, 2014 at [[http://www3.imperial.ac.uk/campusinfo/southkensington|Imperial College]] in London, UK. [[https://www.regonline.com/Register/Checkin.aspx?EventID=1569519|Registration for CAC 2014]] includes and covers attendance at all OGF 42 co-located sessions.

Other co-located conferences for this event include the [[http://www.iis.ee.imperial.ac.uk/saso2014/|8th IEEE International Conference on Self-Adaptive and Self-Organizing Systems (SASO 2014)]] and the [[http://www.p2p-conference.org/p2p14/|14th IEEE International Conference on Peer-to-Peer Computing (P2P’14)]]. Many activities including the keynote, opening reception, and conference dinner are also in common with the full set of co-located conferences as described above. 

See the full set of {{:events:ogf-42:fas_2014_conference_booklets.pdf|FAS* conference booklets}} for details like event maps, locations etc., and please note that this includes updates from [[events:ogf-42:schedule|online schedule linked here]], is the most up to date for OGF, and takes precedence over the listings in the printed booklet.

In addition to the other activities at these conferences, OGF will host its own BOF and tutorial sessions, working group and community implementation group meetings on topics related to OGF technologies in a wide variety of areas in advanced distributed computing. 

OGF is co-sponsoring the [[http://htcaas.kisti.re.kr/wiki/index.php/AMGCC14|2nd International Workshop on Autonomic Management of high performance Grid and Cloud Computing (AMGCC’14)]] co-located there, to take place on Friday, September 12, 2014, and note that the CAC 2014 conference also includes the [[http://sesar.di.unimi.it/ACC2014/|2014 International Workshop on Autonomic Cloud Cybersecurity (ACC '14)]] to take place Monday, September 8.

Many OGF group sessions have been planned, including meetings of the DRMAA, OCCI, JSDL, BES, CAOPS, VOMS-PROC and IDEL working groups and the Federated Security community group, a tutorial and demonstration for the DFDL standard, an OGF Town Hall and a future events planning session, a BoF on "Use of Cloud and Container Technologies in Grids", along with the other co-located conference poster session, workshops, reception and conference dinner. For a full list of OGF events including these co-located activities, see the [[events:ogf-42:schedule|OGF 42 Schedule]] page. 

If you have not already done so, please [[https://www.regonline.com/Register/Checkin.aspx?EventID=1569519|register]] for this event as soon as possible!

(*) Note that this time, the OGF NSI working group and its associated activities will be held two weeks later at the [[https://events.nordu.net/display/NORDU2014/Programme|Nordunet 2014]] conference rather than at OGF 42 itself.
