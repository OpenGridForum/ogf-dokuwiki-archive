I'm not sure the best way to go about this. The benefit of a mailing 
list is that everyone sees what's going on (especially those with more 
peripheral interest) and it's immediately obvious when new information 
appears. The downside is that it's very much unstructured. The wiki has 
the opposite benefits and problems.

Does anyone have experience in both? In mine, wiki's haven't provided 
good areas for discussion, but mailing list stuff seems to never make it 
into the wiki.
