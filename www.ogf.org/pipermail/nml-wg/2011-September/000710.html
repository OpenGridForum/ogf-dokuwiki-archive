All,

Let me give a short report on the status of the URN:OGF:network
identifier registration.

This registrations involves three documents:

1. Delegation of the urn:ogf namespace to the OGF (this was not the case
yet).
2. Put a OGF procedure in place for the registration of subnamespace
identifiers.
3. Register urn:ogf:network using this procedure.

Last week, the IESG approved the urn:ogf registration, and today IANA
registered the namespaces identifier at
http://www.iana.org/assignments/urn-namespaces/urn-namespaces.txt.
It is expected that the associated RFC will be published within 60 days
(it is waiting for details of the following normative reference).

It is expected that the document which described the OGF procedure will
go in 60-day public comment next week (it is now in GFSG review). After
that, it will receive one final GFSG last call before it is published.

I'll report on the urn:ogf:network document later.
