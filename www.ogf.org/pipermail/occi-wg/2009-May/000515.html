Dear OCCI list members,

In some of the discussions about implementation, I see that several  
people stated their interest in contributing use cases and  
requirements. Please do not forget that you are invited to do so by  
including new use cases (the use case could be a simple one-line  
description) at http://forge.ogf.org/sf/wiki/do/viewPage/projects.occi-wg/wiki/UseCases 
  or even new requirement sources at http://forge.ogf.org/sf/wiki/do/viewPage/projects.occi-wg/wiki/Requirements

We are also in the process of extracting the requirements for the use  
case descriptions:

http://forge.ogf.org/sf/wiki/do/viewPage/projects.occi-wg/wiki/RequirementsExtractedFromUseCaseDescriptions
