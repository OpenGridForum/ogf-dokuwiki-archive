I apologize for my intermission but I would give my grain of sand. I
think that the "International System of Units" should be used and the
representation should support this kind of numbers, for example, support
units like: "1 bit", "1 kbit", "1 Gb", "1 GiB", "1 Ghz", etc. Don't
forget that "kilo-" is represented by "k" (not "K") and the difference
between "Gb" (gigabit) and "GiB" (gibibyte, giga-binary-byte).
