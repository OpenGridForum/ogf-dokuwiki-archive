Folks,

 

As we discussed in the HPC Profile session yesterday, Marvin Theimer must
step down as the co-chair of the HPC Profile WG because of his new job
responsibilities. (Note that I have been assured that this by no means
implies that Microsoft will reduce or eliminate their effort in our WG).
