Hi Duane

here are the notes I made at OGF20 when attending the session. Perhaps 
this could be discussed tonight as well

The profile is only half a fix to the real problem since it will only 
allow a grid service provider (SP) to know who you are, without knowing 
what you are entitled to do. However, it should not be too difficult to 
extend the current work to provide a fuller more complete solution, by 
borrowing some of the ideas used by Microsoft Cardspace and Trust 
Negotiation, in which SP’s publish the authorisation tokens that they 
require, and IDPs publish the authz tokens they can issue. In this way a 
user should be able to easily marry the two together in order to ensure 
that he can get access to the resources he is entitled to.

regards

David
