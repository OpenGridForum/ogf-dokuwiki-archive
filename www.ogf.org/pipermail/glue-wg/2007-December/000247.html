There is 3rd option, similar to B but simpler, where the AdminDomainID
appears directly in each Service:

<Grid>
    <AdminDomain>
          <ID>A</ID>
          <Name>...</Name>
           ...
    </AdminDomain>
    <Service>
           <ID>B</ID>
           <AdminDomainID>A<AdminDomainID>
           <Name>..</Name>
           <Endpoint>
                 <ID>F</ID>
           </Endpoint>
    </Service>
    <Service>
           <ID>C</ID>
           <AdminDomainID>A<AdminDomainID>
           <Name>..</Name>
           <Endpoint>

     </Service>
</Grid>

Could this be considered?
