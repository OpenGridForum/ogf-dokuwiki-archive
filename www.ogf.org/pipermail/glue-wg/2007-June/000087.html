> properties of the execution host"? At the moment you seem to have a
> mixture of styles.

one requirements for use cases is that they should be "testable", that 
is it should be possible to verify if the schema satisfies them.

Therefore, general use case stating "user wants to know the properties 
of the execution host" is somewhat incomplete. It should be refined by 
adding the list of properties that are interesting for a user.

The fact that at the moment there is a mixture of styles is because some 
of them were meant to be placeholders to be later refined.
