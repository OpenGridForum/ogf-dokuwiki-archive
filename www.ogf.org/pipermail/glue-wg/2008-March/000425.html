Hi,

I have been looking at the current draft of GLUE 2.0 to see how our 
existing infrastructure deployed on GLUE 1.2 (with APACGrid extensions) 
would move to GLUE 2.0, and I have realized that the LRMSType_t type used 
for the LRMSType field lists only two schedulers - "openpbs" and "lsf" - 
even though there is a number of others used around ("LoadLeveler", "SGE", 
"Torque" ... and few other flavours of PBS, which might have an entry on 
its own).

I see that the field is typed as an Open enumeration, and at the 
syntactical level, it may not be necessary to list all the possible values 
in the specification.  But I think that the widely used schedulers should 
be included - what's the general opinion on this?


Thanks in advance for the feedback.
