here is an updated schedule for GLUE calls for this and the following week:

    * 14 Mar 2008 - 11 AM CET - 1,5h - storage
    * 14 Mar 2008 - 3 PM CET - 1,5h - computing
    * 17 Mar 2008 - 4 PM CET - 1,5h - implementation
    * 18 Mar 2008 - 2 PM CET - 1,5h - storage
    * 18 Mar 2008 - 4 PM CET - 1,5h - computing

https://forge.ogf.org/sf/wiki/do/viewPage/projects.glue-wg/wiki/PhoneMeetings

afternoon, while the third in a different day.
