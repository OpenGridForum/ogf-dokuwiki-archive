The 16th Global Grid Forum - GGF16
February 13-16, 2006
Athens, Greece
Hosted by Greek Research and Technology Network (GRNET)

GGF16 Welcome Dinner
The GGF16 Welcome Dinner will take place on Monday, February 13, beginning at 7:30pm at the Aegli in Athens, one of the most historic meeting places in Athens since the early 1900s. Aegli has been part of the lives of the Athenians for the past two centuries. It is situated within the Zappion Gardens, a unique garden inspired from the Versailles Gardens in Paris, a place where
the residents of the city have always chosen to take their daily stroll. This activity was combined with a refreshment or coffee break at Aegli, a familiar meeting point where the visitors had the opportunity to attend the various cultural events that were hosted regularly.
