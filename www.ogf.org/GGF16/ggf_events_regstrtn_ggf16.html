The Next Event

Registration

The 16th Global Grid Forum - GGF16
February 13-16, 2006
Athens, Greece
Hosted by Greek Research and Technology Network (GRNET)

Registration Fees

GGF16 Registration gives you access to the entire meeting, including the Regional Plenary Sessions, Community Sessions, Standards Sessions, BoFs, Workshops and the Welcome Dinner.

GGF16 One-Day Pass Registration gives you access to GGF16 meeting activities on your specific registered day (Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday or Thursday). Multiple One-Day Passes are not accepted. Please check our schedule to find the day that is of most interest to you.

GGF16 Student Registration gives you access to the entire meeting, , including the Regional Plenary Sessions, Community Sessions, Standards Sessions, BoFs, Workshops and the Welcome Dinner.

Onsight Fax Registration Form

GGF16 Guest Registration is available for $55.00 per guest for the Welcome Dinner on Monday, February 13. For more information about the Welcome Dinner, please see our Special Events Page. You may register your guest during the online registration process or use the faxable guest registration form.

For your convenience, badge and conference material will be handed out onsite. Please see the following onsite registration hours:

Onsite Registration Hours:

Sunday, February 12, 7:00pm-9:00pm
Monday, February 13, 7:30am-5:30pm
Tuesday, February 14, 7:30am-5:30pm
Wednesday, February 15, 8:00am-4:00pm
Thursday, February 16, 8:00am-1:00pm


CANCELLATION POLICY:
- All cancellations must be confirmed in writing and sent to registration@ggf.org
- All "GGF16" Registrations are subject to a $100USD cancellation fee
- All "GGF16 One Day Pass" Registrations to a $50 cancellation fee
- Cancellation Deadline is February 3, 2006
