GGF7 - The Seventh Global Grid Forum
"Grids Around the World"
March 4 - 7, 2003
Tokyo, Japan
Hotel Intercontinental Keio Plaza

The meeting gathered ~780 grid enthusiasts, practitioners and experts for the first GGF held in the Asia-Pacific.

GGF7 Presentations:

  • Plenary Keynote - A Grid Research Perspective
    • Building an Open Grid  Presenter(s): Dr. Ian Foster, Argonne National Laboratory and The University of Chicago
  • Technology Update Panel
    • OGSA: Status and Experiences Presenter(s): Steve Tuecke
  • Panel on Funding Updates on Public Sector Grid Projects
    • Grid Projects in Japan Presenter(s): Hiroshi Sugiyama
    • Funding Updates - EU Research Programme on Grids Presenter(s): Kyriakos Baxevanidis
  • The Nominating Committee (NomCom) Process, to repopulate positions on the Steering Group Presented during town hall meeting
  • Grid Application Updates
    • NFS Programs Supporting Grid Computing Research and Infrastructure Presenter(s): Dr. Frederica Darema
    • The UK e-Science Programme: Second Phase Presenter(s): Tony Hey
    • National Research Grid Initiative (NAREGI) Project: FY'03 - FY'07 Presenter(s): Kenichi Miura
