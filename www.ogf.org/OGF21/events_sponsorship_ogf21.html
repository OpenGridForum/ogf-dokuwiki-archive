The 21st Open Grid Forum - OGF21
October 15-19, 2007
Grand Hyatt Seattle
Seattle, Washington

Sponsorship opportunities are available for OGF21 taking place October 15 through 19, 2007 at the Grand Hyatt Hotel in Seattle, Washington.   At this event over 300 grid leaders, technologists and existing users will come together to develop Grid standards and workshop best practices.  This is a great opportunity for organizations to showcase their products and services, to communicate their brand and to gain exposure with a wide spectrum of stakeholders, leaders and decision makers in the grid community.

SPONSORSHIP BENEFITS:
  • Reach a highly qualified audience of Grid technical leaders, researchers, users, executives, decision makers and purchasing influencers who will be engaging in supplier research and education
  • Build brand awareness and showcase products and services through conference marketing and signage and in the sponsor display area
  • Position your organization as a Grid thought leader and innovator
  • Present your open-source and commercially available grid software during the Software Forum sessions
  • Interact with grid builders who share their grid case studies
  • Generate leads by reaching key decision makers in the buying cycle
  • Network and make connections at sessions and social events
  • Gain exposure to OGF community and achieve good will by showing your support for OGF
 
OVERVIEW OF SPONSORSHIP OPPORTUNITIES

DISPLAY TABLES

This premier sponsorship will belong to an exclusive leader in grid.  As the only sponsor in this prestigious category, your company will be recognized as a market leader and be able to benefit from the association with OGF.  Your company name and logo will be seen, heard and worn by each participant and prospect.
