The following section presents a chosen set of standards in the field of job control and monitoring in \gls{drm} systems, which can serve as starting point for a cloud batch processing standardization attempt. We focus solely on the \gls{ogf} standardization body in this summary, since all relevant activities in the area of cluster and grid systems happen in this organization.
