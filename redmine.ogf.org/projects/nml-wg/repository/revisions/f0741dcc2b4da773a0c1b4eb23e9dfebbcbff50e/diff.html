\section{NML Base Schema}%


  		





  
  4
  
    
\label{s:schema}


  		





  4
  5
  
    

  		





  5
  
  
    
The NML Base schema describes an information model with elements and their


  		





  6
  
  
    
relations that describe computer networks. This schema is kept intentionally


  		





  7
  
  
    
general, with provisions to extend the schema to describe layer-specific


  		





  8
  
  
    
information.


  		





  
  6
  
    
The NML Base schema describes an information model that describe computer networks. 


  		





  
  7
  
    
This schema is kept intentionally general, with provisions to extend the schema to 


  		





  
  8
  
    
describe layer-specific information.


  		





  9
  9
  
    

  		





  10
  
  
    
The URI of the all objects, relations and parameters is \texttt{http://http://schemas.ogf.org/nml/2012/10/base\#}.


  		





  
  10
  
    
The schema consists of classes, attributes, relations, parameters and logic. 


  		





  
  11
  
    
Classes describe types of objects. Relations describe the relations between classes. 


  		





  
  12
  
    
Attributes describe properties of classes. Logic describes how some relations may 


  		





  
  13
  
    
be derived from other relations.


  		





  
  14
  
    
Parameters, like attributes, are properties of classes, but may subtly change the logic.


  		





  
  15
  
    

  		





  
  16
  
    
All classes, relations, attributes and parameters defined in this document have an 


  		





  
  17
  
    
identifier within the namespace \texttt{http://schemas.ogf.org/nml/2012/10/base\#}.
