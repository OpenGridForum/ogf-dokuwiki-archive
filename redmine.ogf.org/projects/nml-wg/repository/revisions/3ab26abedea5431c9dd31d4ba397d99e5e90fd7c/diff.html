The Network Markup Language has two different normative syntaxes. The syntaxes are in regular XML, and another in OWL RDF/XML syntax.


  		





  
  597
  
    
The XML syntax is aimed at legacy applications, while the OWL syntax is aimed at more powerful Semantic Web-oriented applications. These syntaxes are defined in Appendices \ref{s:xmlschema} and \ref{s:owlschema} respectively.
