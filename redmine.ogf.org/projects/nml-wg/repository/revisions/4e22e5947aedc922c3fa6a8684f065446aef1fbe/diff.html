  473
  
    
A \emph{Label} is the technology-specific value that distinguishes a single data stream (a channel) embedded in a larger data stream. The \emph{Label} can either be a resource label (with one value), or a pair of source and destination labels (with two values)~\cite{G800}. Examples of resource labels are a VLAN number, wavelength, et cetera.
