\emph{Relation}s describe how different\emph{Network Object}s relate to each other, typically to be combined to form a network topology description. The relations have been described above, but for ease of reference we also give a full list and definition here (in alphabetical order). In principle a \emph{Relation} can go from any object to any other object. The list below includes definitions for a subset of the possible relations. If a particular \emph{Relation} between two \emph{Network Objects} is not listed below, it is undefined.
