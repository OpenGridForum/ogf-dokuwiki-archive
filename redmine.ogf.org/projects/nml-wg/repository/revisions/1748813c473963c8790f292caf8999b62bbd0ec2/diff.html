  12
  
  
    
Attributes describe properties of classes. Logic describes how some relations may 


  		





  13

  14

  12

  13
  
    
Attributes describe properties of classes.


  		





  
  14

  15

  16

\subsubsection{Network Object}\label{network_object}


  		





  34
  38
  
    

  		





  35
  39
  
    
The basic abstract class of the schema is the \emph{Network Object}.  Most classes inherit from it.


  		





  36
  40
  
    

  		





  37
  
  
    
The \emph{Network Object} can be associated with a \emph{Location} using the \emph{locatedAt} attribute, can be related to other instances via \emph{Relation}s and can have a \emph{Lifetime}.  Every Network Object \MUST{} have an \emph{id} attribute, which \MUST{} be a unique URI.  


  		





  38
  
  
    
These characteristics are inherited by the subclasses of the \emph{Network Object} class.  


  		





  39
