GIN Interoperation Scenarios Demonstrations

ATTENTION: Just in planning state - nothing is set and finally decided yet

Location & Dates

Supercomputing 2007 November 10-16, 2007
Reno-Sparks Convention Center, Reno, USA
http://sc07.supercomputing.org/

GIN members that plan to participate in the SC2007

Some members of GIN will personally participate at the SC2007.

More information

Booth that are able to demonstrate GIN efforts

Some members of GIN and their organizations will have a booth at the SC2007.

More information

Planned Demonstrations

GINSC2007-001: HPC-P (OGSA-BES+JSDL+JSDL-HPC Application Extensions) demonstrations (Organized by HPC-P Group)

These demonstrations are managed by the High Performance Computing Profile WG (HPC-P-WG).

Area: GIN-JOBS
Category: Future Solutions
Status: evolving

More information

GINSC2007-002: SRB/SRM island interoperabilities (GO!)

Plans on GIN-DATA list to achieve interoperability using the SRM interface with SRB.

Area: GIN-DATA
Category: Production
Status: Precise plans

More information

GINSC2007-003: ByteIO Transfer (Organized by ByteIO Group)

Plan to provide ByteIO demonstrations

Area: GIN-DATA
Category: Future Solutions
Status: To be discussed in more detail

More information

GINSC2007-004: Information Services (GO!)

Laurence Field is in charge of presenting the unified information of numerous production Grids on a Google Map.

Area: GIN-INFO
Category: Production
Status: stable demo

More information

Demonstrations related to the Security?

Area: GIN-AUTH
Category: Production
Status: To be discussed

More information

GINSC2007-006: GIN Portals (GO!)

PGrade Portals and GridSphere portals demonstrations accessing production Grid software.

Area: GIN-JOBS
Category: Production
Status: To be discussed

More information

GINSC2007-007: OGSA Profiles Interops (out)

Demonstrations about OGSA interoperation efforts (especially profiles)

Area: GIN-JOBS
Category: Future Solutions
Status: cancelled

More information

GINSC2007-008: OGSA-BES with GLUE (out)

Using OGSA-BES endpoints in combination with GLUE.

Area: GIN-JOBS, GIN-INFO
Category: Future Solutions
Status: cancelled

More information

GINSC2007-009: UNICORE and gLite Interoperation (GO!)

Submit Jobs from EGEE (via gLite) to DEISA (UNICORE) used in production.

Area: GIN-JOBS
Category: Production
Status: Stable demo

More information

GINSC2007-010: DEISA and Australian Grid Interop (GO!)

Demonstration of a small job workflow between DEISA and Australian Grid (Monash).

Area: GIN-JOBS
Category: Production
Status: Available for SC2007

More information

GINSC2007-011: VOMS-enabled and OGSA-BES based job submits between UNICORE and gLite (GO!)

Demonstration of OGSA-BES-based job submissions between UNICORE and gLite that are both provide authorization based on SAML VOMS.

Area: GIN-JOBS, GIN-AUTH
Category: Future Solutions
Status: Available for SC2007

More information

GINSC2007-012: OGSA-RUS based accounting information exchange between gLite (DGAS), Globus (SGAS), and UNICORE (GO!)

Demonstration of OGSA-RUS-based exchange of accounting information (OGF URF) between DGAS, SGAS and UNICORE.

Area: GIN-OPS
Category: Future Solutions
Status: Available for SC2007

More information

GINSC2007-013: DAIR Demo (GO!)

A demonstration with the DAI-R standard.

Area: GIN-DATA
Category: Future Solutions
Status: Proposed

More information

GINSC2007-014: GRAAP (out)

Evolving demonstrations of interops with WS-Agreement specification.

Area: GIN-OPS
Category: Future Solutions
Status: To be discussed

More information

GINSC2007-015: GridFTP2 Demo (GO!)

The GridFTP2 protocol as defined in GFD.47 is by now implemented in dCache and Globus C and Java client libraries.

Area: GIN-DATA
Category: Production
Status: Planned
