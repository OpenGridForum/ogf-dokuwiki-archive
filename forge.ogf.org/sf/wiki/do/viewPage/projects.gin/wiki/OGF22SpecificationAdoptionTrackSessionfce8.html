OGF22 - GIN Specification Adoption Track Session

This session at OGF22 will basically describe which standards have been used in our interoperation/interoperability demonstrations and will provide hints where standards are necessary to facilitate interoperability. The major outcome of this session should be interesting discussions between e-Science/Grid infrastructures on the one hand and e-Science/Grid technologies on the other.
