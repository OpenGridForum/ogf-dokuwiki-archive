GIN Wiki

GIN has 5 current focus teams working on describing a few islands of basic interoperable services on participating production Grids. Each of the focus teams has a Wiki thread attached to the group name for further information in that area.

  • GIN-auth - Authorization and Identity Management
  • GIN-data - Data Management and Movement
  • GIN-jobs - Job Description and Submission
  • GIN-info - Information Services and Schema
  • GIN-ops - Operations experience of pilot test applications

For the moment, the previous GIN Wiki at: http://wiki.nesc.ac.uk/read/gin-jobs is also active and may contain information of interest.

The resources on the list here have been certified by their home Grids as being compliant with at least one of the GIN Phase I interoperable services. Users who already have access to participating resources may find success in using these resources, but since we are still in early stages of interoperation testing, this is not yet to be expected.

Interested individuals may also subscribe to the gin@ggf.org mailing list or one of the sub-group mailing lists: by sending a mail with the body subscribe <list name> to majordomo@ggf.org

Mailing list archives can be found here:

  • gin-data: (date, thread)
