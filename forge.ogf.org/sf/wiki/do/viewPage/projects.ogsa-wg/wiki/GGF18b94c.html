<3> OGSA and EGA Reference Model

  • Date: Tuesday, September 12
  • Time: 4:00 pm - 5:30 pm
  • Location: 159A-B
  • Owner: Tom Maguire and Hiro Kishimoto
  • description
    • OGSA-WG and EGA Reference Model WG have a dialogue on OGSA architecture and EGA reference model. Both parties are hoping to achieve beneficial synergy effects.
    • Before creating possible reconciliation or resolution, both agreed to identify similarities and differences between our existing works.
  • agenda
    • Brief high level summary of EGA Ref Model (Bob Thome and Paul Strong, 20 minutes)
    • Proposals for work
      • Glossary comparison (Jem Treadwell, 15 minutes)
        • Identify similarities and differences between terminology.
      • ERG Ref Model and CDDLM (Stuart Schaefer, 15 minutes)
        • Identify similarities and differences between EGA Ref Model's Dependency Graph & Life Cycle of Grid Components and CDDLM-WG's work.
    • Open discussion (40 minutes)
      • Work plan (new design team?)
  • Invitation accepted
    • EGA RM-WG (Bob Thome, Dave Pearson, Paul Strong, Parviz Peiravi (by phone))
    • CDDLM-WG(Dejan Milojicic, Stuart Schaefer, Satish Bhat)
