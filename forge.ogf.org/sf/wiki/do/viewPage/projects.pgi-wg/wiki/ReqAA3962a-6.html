Req. Nb ID Description Source Areas Dependencies Status Date
AA3.5 17 For Client authentication, Services must accept all following authentication methods: Full X509, X509 Proxy General Security AA3 Agreed YES 2010-06-22

Etienne Urbah on 2010-03-17

  • Propose requirement with title  'For Client authentication, Services MUST accept BOTH RFC-3820-compliant X509 proxies and SAML assertions'

Luigi's comment on 2010-04-14

  • perhaps conflicts with AA3.2, AA3.3. A service MUST or MAY accept BOTH RFC-3820-compliant X509 proxies and SAML assertions?

Morris, Balazs and Etienne on 2010-04-21

  • Redefined title, because a more general statement of variety of authentication methods was necessary :  'For Client authentication, Services must accept a certain number of authentication methods'
  • Source = General
  • Spreadsheet ID = 17

Amsterdam meeting on 2010-04-28

  • Open  (clear, but NO agreement found yet)

Chicago meeting on 2010-06-22

  • Change title to 'For Client authentication, Services must accept all following authentication methods: Full X509, X509 Proxy'
  • Agreed YES
