wiki1460: SMISANSIINCITS3882004v1.0.2

Standard

1. The name of the specification

SMI-S(ANSI INCITS 388-2004)

2. A short statement (<100 words) of the purpose and function of the specification

This Technical Specification defines the a method for the interoperable management of a heterogeneous Storage Area Network (SAN). This Technical Specification describes the information available to a WBEM Client from an SMI-S compliant CIM Server. This Technical Specification describes an object-oriented, XML-based, messaging-based interface designed to support the specific requirements of managing devices in and through Storage Area Networks (SANs).
