Gridforum Chairs

Introduction

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Keep information for your group current in the Living Charter Interface.
    (changes trigger AD approval, and are reflected in GF after approval).
  • Use the same Living Charter Interface to propose a new group.
  • Hold a session at an OGF meeting
    • Request a session at the next OGF meeting
    • Manage your group's session at an OGF meeting
