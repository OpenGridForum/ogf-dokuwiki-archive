Gridforge How-To

Get a Gridforge Login

Obtaining a Gridforge login is a self-serve affair. You can create your own account, you'll just need a valid email address for confirmation.

Become a member of a Gridforge group

Each group (called a "project" in Gridforge) has a home page with a button "Join this project" on it. You can get a list of all the projects by clicking the "Projects" link at the very top of the page.

Contribute to a Group's Wiki

To contribute to the wiki for a particular group, you need to have a Gridforge login (see above). For some group's wikis (like this one), you may only need to be logged into Gridforge to contribute. For others, you may need to become a member of the group (see above).
