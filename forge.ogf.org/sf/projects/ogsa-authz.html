WG drafts in public call - OGSA-AUTHZ-WG
The four drafts of the working group are now in public call and should be completed prior to the OGF24 meeting in Singapore. To refresh your memory these are:

1. Functional Components of Grid Service Provider Authorisation Service Middleware (pub 6 April 08)
2. Use of WS-TRUST and SAML to access a Credential Validation Service (pub 9 July 08)
3. Use of XACML Request Context to Obtain an Authorisation Decision (pub 31 Mar 08)
4. Use of SAML to retrieve Authorization Credentials (pub 7 April 2008)
